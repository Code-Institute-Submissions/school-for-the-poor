Meet our teachers

We have a large number of qualified EFL teachers in our schoools. All of our teachers have a passion for learning and sharing their knowledge with others. We pride ourselves on the enthusiasm and ingenuity of our staff to present our course content in innovative and exciting ways. This way we keep our students fully engaged and learning without even realising. We also have several native English speaking teachers for those students who may want to focus on pronunciation and more nuanced language points. Meet our Senior Teaching Team below, and hover over the photos to find out their favousite hobbies.
